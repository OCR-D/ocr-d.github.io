Ligatures (Level 1 and 2)

For transcriptions at levels 1 and 2 ligatures resulting from the printing technology (consonant ligatures) are always split up. The information ligature is documented by the attribute ligaturewith the specification true.

<Glyph id="'Angabe einer eindeutigen ID'" ligature="true">
