Book decoration, drawings (GraphicRegion)

A graphicRegion is used to mark simple graphics, handwritten notes and perforations, among other things. The following elements are considered to be a simple graphic:
  • logo
  • letterhead with graphic design (letterhead)
  • decoration
  • frame
  • handwritten-annotation
  • stamp
  • signature
  • barcode
  • paper-grow
  • punch-hole
  • other
