First Step : The Page Types

From a structural point of view, a book consists of different page types. The pages to be registered must be marked on the basis of the following typology.
Page types according to the PageXml schema:
  • front-cover
  • back-cover
  • title page: title
  • table-of-contents
  • index
  • text of the book: content
  • blank page : blank
  • other
