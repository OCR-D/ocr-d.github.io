Page Margin

The page margin delimits the entire page (including column headers, page numbers and sheet signatures) from parts that do not belong to the text (including handwritten additions) or the whitespace. Die Markierung des Seitenrandes ist dem Satzspiegel vorzuziehen.

Example with handwritten additions. This example also shows a particularity. In this case the addition "1885" is included within the page.
